61lb Chinook - Haida Gwaii

The highlight of my fishing career right here, the largest salmon ive ever guided, if i could relive any 10 seconds of my life it would be these ones.

The Perfect Halibut

Most all halibut over about 60lbs are females, so to keep the stock healthy, any halibut over 133cm in length must be released. This beautiful fish of BC gold was exactly that. Weighing in at 59lbs!

42lb Salmon - Haida Gwaii

This salmon was fought out in the open waters of the pacific on a flat calm day with humpback whales feeding all around us. The sun, the whales, and the fish made for an unforgettable experience.
